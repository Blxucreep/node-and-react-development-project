Getting Started

Learn how to get started with Anki and set up your study materials.

To get started with Anki:

  1. Download and install Anki on your device.
  2. Explore Lesson: you can edit lesson or add lesson fact.
  3. Study flashcard and when you think you have learn it click on the button.

Features

Discover the features and functionalities of Anki that can enhance your learning experience.

  • Discover flashcard:A wide range of lesson and facts at your disposition and a glossary for important defintion.
  • Customizable Flashcards: Create flashcards with rich text formatting to suit your study needs.
  • Progress: You can see your statistics in time and number of lesson learning but also your achievement, so you can learn with fun and challenge with your friends !
  • Import and Export: Import existing flashcard decks or export your decks for sharing with others.
  • Support:If you have any issues you can contact our support by email,mobile or chat.

Usage

Learn how to effectively use Anki and optimize your study sessions.

Here are some tips for using Anki effectively:

  • Review your flashcards regularly to reinforce your knowledge and prevent forgetting.
  • Create custom note types to organize different types of flashcards and optimize your study workflow.
  • Use tags to categorize and filter your flashcards based on topics, difficulty levels, or any other criteria.
  • Take advantage of Anki's statistics and progress tracking to monitor your learning progress and identify areas that need more focus.

FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about Anki.

Q: Can I use Anki offline?

A: Yes, Anki allows you to study your flashcards offline once they have been synchronized to your device.

Q: Can I see my progression in Anki?

A: Yes, you can see it on progress statistics.

Q: Can I have some trophee in Anki?

A: Yes, you can see it on achievement page but the real trophee is the knowledge.

Q: Can I share my flashcard decks with others?

A: Yes, you can export your flashcard decks and share them with other Anki users.
